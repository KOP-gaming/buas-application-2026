TUTORIAL 1: The Button
- text: "this is a puzzle room, in these rooms you have to find a way to press the button to open the door to the next room"
- objective: "press the button to open the door"
LAYOUT
- simple box
- fixed camera
- single button in the middle
- a way to cross the map without going on the button
- level should feel like it is inside a testing facility or lab
GAMEPLAY
player walks onto/over the button to activate the door, when the player crosses the button the door light turns green and the player can continue.

TUTORIAL 2: Shoot

- objective: "press E to shoot"
LAYOUT
- same map as TUTORIAL 1 but the button is now protected by destroyable boxes
- fixed camera
- single button in the middle
- level should feel like it is inside of a testing facility/labn
